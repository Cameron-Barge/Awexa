what is awexa?

the

problem

Parents want to use technology to guide and manage household tasks without having to give their kids electronic devices.

Current solutions are generally mobile/web app-based and can not provide smart interaction with kids through smart home technology.

Alexa can provide a smart interface to guide and manage kids through smart reminders, progress updates and incentivising verbal communication.

learn more

our

solution

Awexa exists as a multi-platform application for managing and interfacing with kids in regards to household chores.

It primarily relies on parents using the Amazon Echo to track chore progress and trends, and update new tasks and rewards.

Awexa is perfect for households with an Amazon Echo wanting a way to improve household activities through smart home technology instead of mobile device micromanagement.
